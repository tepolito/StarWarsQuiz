Star Wars Quiz

Welcome to the Star Wars Quiz App!!

This app will quiz you on your knowledge of Star Wars. It is pretty straight forward. Once you click begin you will be promted with a question. Click on the answer you believe to be correct. The app will tell you if you got it right or wrong then update your score accordingly. When you are ready for the next question hit the next button and you will recieve a new question. At the very end, hit the reset button to give it another try if so desired.
